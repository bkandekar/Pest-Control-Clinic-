Protecting Kolhapur Families with Eco-Friendly Care

Founded over 15 years ago, Pest Control Clinic was established to elevate standard pest management. Recognizing that traditional pest spraying can introduce hazardous chemical odors, we pioneered "Clinical-Precision Pest Management". We utilize low-toxicity, odorless, WHO-approved target gels and biological sprays that eliminate pests while protecting family health.

Headquartered in Savarde, Kolhapur, we have protected over 12,000 households and premium commercial buildings from severe structural termite damage and pathogen-carrying insects. We take a complete health and sanitization approach, treating your home with the same sanitary detail as an operating theater.
