Common Safety & Treatment Queries

We believe in transparent, scientifically backable solutions. Read our answers to the top 15 queries asked by Kolhapur families.

Is your cockroach gel treatment really 100% odorless?

Yes, absolutely. Our advanced cockroach gel baits utilize WHO-certified food-grade attractants that emit zero chemical odor, fumes, or volatile organic compounds (VOCs). You can remain in the kitchen, cook food, and sleep comfortably in your house during and immediately after the treatment with zero disruption.

Do I need to empty my kitchen cupboards before the gel treatment?

No, there is no need to empty your modular kitchen drawers, shelves, or cabinets. Since we apply tiny, targeted droplets of odorless gel in corner hinges, tracks, and crevice boundaries using a micro-applicator syringe, your utensils and food items can remain safely untouched in their places.

Are your pest control chemicals safe for infants and toddlers?

Yes, all our gel formulations are completely safe. They are non-toxic, placed in hidden cavities, hinges, and behind appliances where toddlers cannot reach or touch them. For general wet sprays in wall corners, we recommend keeping children away from the treated room for 1 to 2 hours until the mist has dried completely.

Are your pest control treatments safe for household dogs and cats?

Yes. We use advanced pet-safe formulations with exceptionally low mammalian toxicity profiles. Our baits are designed to target specific pest nervous systems with zero impact on mammals (like dogs and cats). For security, our rodent bait stations are heavily secured and lock-protected so pets cannot bite them open.

What is your "Drill-Fill-Seal" termite treatment warranty?

Our industry-standard Drill-Fill-Seal termite barrier treatment comes with a comprehensive 1-year or 5-year written service warranty (depending on your plan). If subterranean termites reappear or damage any treated wood structures during your contract, our emergency operators will re-treat your home at 100% zero cost.

Do you offer natural, chemical-free Ayurvedic treatments?

Yes! For eco-conscious homes, we offer a specialized Ayurvedic Eco-Repellent treatment. We utilize botanical essential oils (such as neem, eucalyptus, and lemongrass extracts) that act as highly effective, natural repellent barriers against mosquitoes, flies, and silverfish without any synthetic chemical footprint.

How do you protect homes from mosquito swarms during monsoons?

We employ a dual action strategy. First, we treat stagnant water pockets using bio-larvicides to destroy mosquito larvae before they hatch. Second, we apply synthetic pyrethroid wall mists along dark corners and dense foliage in your yard, creating an invisible, long-lasting repellent barrier that kills adult mosquitoes on contact.

Can you perform outdoor treatments on a rainy day?

For rain protection, our indoor gel and barrier treatments are completely unaffected. For outdoor mosquito or garden boundary sprays, we coordinate with you to schedule on a dry day, or we use specialized oil-based micro-encapsulated formulations that do not wash away easily in light drizzle.

How long does it take to see results after cockroach treatment?

Our cockroach bait gels trigger a domino effect. Within 24 to 48 hours, feeding cockroaches carry the odorless bait back to their dark nesting colonies, which starts eradicating the entire population. You will see a dramatic 90% reduction in pest sight within 4 to 5 days, and complete nest elimination within 2 weeks.

How often do you run checks under the Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC)?

Our standard AMC package includes 4 scheduled maintenance checks per year (once every quarter). During each visit, our operator runs a comprehensive inspection, replenishes cockroach gel baits, checks termite monitors, and applies a fresh protective boundary shield to guarantee year-round relief.

What are the early signs of a subterranean termite infestation?

Look for mud tubes crawling up concrete foundation walls, hollow clicking sounds inside wooden door frames, sticking or hard-to-open window shutters, and fine, sand-like droppings or discarded wings around floor corners. If you notice any of these, schedule an inspection immediately to prevent structural damage.

Are your treatments safe for elderly family members with asthma?

Yes. Since our gel baits and Ayurvedic options emit no chemical vapors, strong odors, or micro-dust particles, they are 100% safe for elderly patients with asthma, bronchitis, or other respiratory sensitivities. For wet boundary sprays, we simply recommend they relax in another room for 2 hours during application.

Do you provide professional pest control audit logs for businesses?

Yes, we do. For hotels, food processing plants, warehouses, and offices, we issue certified compliance reports, MSDS (Material Safety Data Sheets) logs, and physical service cards that satisfy FDA, FSSAI, and local health department municipal inspection guidelines.

How do you eradicate tough bed bug outbreaks?

We use a rigorous 2-step clinical approach. First, we apply high-density steam treatment directly to mattress seams, wooden headboards, and cracks to kill bed bugs and their microscopic eggs on contact. Second, we apply a safe, odorless dry residual spray along wall borders to prevent any secondary hatching.
